Feature introduced :

AKKA integration with Spring
 - SpringExtension
 - SpringActorProducer
 - AkkaHelper

AKKA actors:
 - NewJournalNotifierActor, actor for listening to newly added journal
 - ScheduledJournalNotifierActor, scheduled actor for notification broadcast. (every 5 minutes)
                                     

Service:
 - EmailService, responsible for sending notification email
 - SubscriptionService,responsible for find Subscriber based on subscribed Category

Repository:
 - SubscriptionRepository, responsible for subscriber data retrieval
